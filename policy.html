Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025/01/03

No Data Collection

Hedera does not collect any personal information from you. All data used by the extension is stored locally within your browser's storage. This data includes, but is not limited to:

  • Browser bookmarks (only if you grant permission)
  • Custom widget configurations
  • Theme preferences

Local Storage Only

Any data generated or used by Hedera is stored exclusively within your browser's local storage. This means:

  • Your data is not accessible by us or any external servers.
  • Your data is not shared with any third parties.
  • Your data remains on your device and under your control.

No Data Transmission

Hedera does not transmit any of your data to our servers or to any third-party servers. All processing and data storage occurs solely within your web browser.

Third-Party Services

Hedera does not integrate with any third-party services unless explicitly requested by you within your browser.
